About Us

Shieldguard Security is a professional indigenous security firm registered by the Ministry of Home Affairs (CPI) in terms of the Private Investigators and Security Guards Control Act (Chapter 27:10) and seeks to be a member of the Security Association of Zimbabwe.

The company seeks to be among the leading providers of premium, top-notch security services in the banking sector, agricultural sector, mining sector, construction sector, transport sector, manufacturing, tourism, education, and medical sector, with a large workforce of guards. Shieldguard is able to provide properly trained and supervised security staff. All security staff has a military, police, or security background.
